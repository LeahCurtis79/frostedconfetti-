Get to know us!

Frosted Cupcake started with just a few friends looking for a way to raise money to give back to the community.

With their combined love for baking, they decided to start with a few flavors of cupcakes and sell to neighbors.

They became so popular with just a few flavors that customers began giving requests for new and unique flavors.

Each week they roll out 4 new flavors while keeping 2 main flavors on the menu.

They money they raise is donated to the local women's and children's shelter to help provide for food and incidentals.
